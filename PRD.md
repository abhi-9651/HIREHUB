1. Problem Statement
Many students struggle to:
Find relevant internships.
Create ATS-friendly resumes.
Identify skill gaps.
Prepare for interviews.
Receive personalized career guidance.
Existing platforms provide job listings but lack personalized AI-driven career support.
HireHub aims to solve this problem through an integrated platform combining internship discovery, resume building, and AI career assistance.

2. Product Vision
To become a one-stop AI-powered career platform that helps students discover opportunities, build strong resumes, and prepare for successful careers.

3. Target Users

Internship seekers
